American Veterans Rebuilding has achieved it's goal of obtaining 501(c)(3) tax exempt status. This means that AVR is now full "go" into fundraising operations. We are currently in process of putting together a video introduction to American Veterans Rebuilding. The video will focus on our mission and what a project looks like from start to finish to include financials. We plan to premier the video here soon. We cannot thank all of you enough for the support to date. Monetary donations will serve as the foundation on which we build. In the coming months, as we prepare to break ground on our first project home, AVR will begin improving its position by investing in legal services, contracting veteran General Contractors and engaging with local technical colleges so that we can fulfill our end of the mission statement. All donations are tax deductible to both AVR and the donor.
