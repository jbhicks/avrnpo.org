THE AVR MISSION

American Veterans Rebuilding is dedicated to the improvement of the American Veteran's Self, Family and Community through Technical Training, Occupational Licensing, Home Ownership Options and Professional Networking.

Technical Training

Participants in AVR construction projects will receive tools and training in the Residential Construction occupation of their choosing.

Occupational Licensing

Participants who complete Technical Training apprenticeship will then receive assistance in acquiring their Occupational Licenses providing them the opportunity of a steady income.

Home Ownership Options

Select participants will be offered the opportunity to purchase AVR project homes at the total construction cost of the project rather than at the inflated market values. They will also be assisted by AVR's growing network of resources to assist them in securing their VA Home Loan and other earned benefits.

Professional Networking

Participants who receive professional trade licenses will rest easy knowing that AVR is covering their six. Professional Networking Services include but are not limited to...

  • Job opportunities outside of AVR project homes.
  • Resources and assistance with claiming their VA benefits.
  • Access to a network of like-minded veterans with similar experiences.
