How Can You Help?

As always, the best thing you can do to support AVR is to follow, like and share our goals on our family of social medias. Sharing our mission through word of mouth is the foundation on which we will build. AVR stands ready to accept the donations needed to begin our first project homes. Please rally as many as possible so that we can succeed from day one. Keep up to date with our progress and growing community at the links below.

The AVR SFC Podcast

The AVR SFC Podcast is dedicated to the improvement of all things regarding the American Veterans' Self Family and Community. Tune in to hear personal accounts of war and its traumas, insights as to what family members of veterans experience, and guidance from community leaders on how to utilized earned benefits.
