Common Issues

Payment was declined by my bank

Banks sometimes decline online transactions for security reasons, especially for first-time donations or if you're traveling. Contact your bank to authorize online donations to American Veterans Rebuilding.

I entered the wrong information

Double-check all payment details including card number, expiration date, security code (CVV), and billing address. Even small errors can cause payment failures.

The page timed out or crashed

If the donation page became unresponsive, please try again with a stable internet connection. Clear your browser cache if problems persist.

I want to donate by check or other method

We accept donations by check, wire transfer, or other methods. Contact us for instructions on alternative donation methods.
