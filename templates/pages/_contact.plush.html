Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer?

Visit our Volunteer page or send a message describing your skills and availability.

Are your services free for veterans?

Yes — our core assistance programs are free to eligible veterans. We may partner with local providers for some services.

How are donations used?

Donations fund veteran programs, training, housing assistance, and administrative costs to keep the organization running. See our donation page for details.
