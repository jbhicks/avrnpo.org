Meet the AVR Team

American Veterans Rebuilding is formed by veterans of the wars in Afghanistan and Iraq. We are soldiers who have lived through hell on earth and found a way to continue to dedicate our lives to the military's core values of loyalty, duty, respect, selfless service, honor, integrity and personal courage.

Founder / President

Michael Price

Michael Price is the Founder and President of American Veterans Rebuilding. He is a father to his daughter Madeline and husband to his wife Jessica. Michael served in the U.S. Army as Active Duty Infantry from 2004 to 2007, forming a brotherhood with Sergio Rubio and Joshua Hicks during a combat tour in Ramadi. He later earned the 37F MOS and served until 2010 as a Psychological Operations Specialist. After separating from the Army in 2010, Michael founded American Veterans Renovations, where he has been improving homes. American Veterans Rebuilding represents the shared vision of the team.

" alt="Sergio Rubio" />
Executive VP

Sergio Rubio

Sergio Rubio is a Registered Nurse with over six years of experience, currently working in the Emergency and Cardiac departments at Kaweah Delta in Southern California. He served his community during the COVID-19 pandemic. Sergio graduated from California State University, Bakersfield in 2018 with a bachelor's degree in Nursing. He served in the U.S. Army for nine years, achieving the rank of Staff Sergeant and completing two combat deployments in Iraq. Sergio brings his expertise to support the mission of American Veterans Rebuilding.

" alt="Joshua Hicks" />
VP / Secretary

Joshua Hicks

Joshua Hicks served as a U.S. Army Infantryman alongside Michael Price for four years, forming a lasting brotherhood. His service included seven years of active duty and one year in the National Guard. Joshua resides in Phoenix, AZ, with his wife Erica and their dog George. He is currently a Software Development Technical Lead at Charles Schwab, Inc.

Treasurer

William Cuccurullo

William Cuccurullo is a manager of financial instruments and real estate, with experience in investments since 2017. He applies his analytical and financial management skills to assess project feasibility and analyze macroeconomic conditions. William graduated from the Zicklin School of Business at Baruch College in New York City with a BBA in finance and marketing. In his spare time, he produces and attends live music events.
