Meet the AVR Team

American Veterans Rebuilding is formed by Combat Veterans of the wars in Afghanistan and Iraq. We are soldiers who have lived through hell on earth and found a way to continue to dedicate our lives to the military's core values of Loyalty, Duty, Respect, Selfless Service, Honor, Integrity and Personal Courage.

Founder / President

Michael Price

Mike is an Operation Iraqi Freedom Combat Veteran (2003-2004) who served in the U.S. Army Military Police. During his deployment Mike served with the 720th Military Police Battalion during the initial invasion of Iraq. His positions included; Tower Guard, Convoy Security, Prisoner Escort, Civil Affairs, and Personal Security Detail for Commanding Officers. Mike has an Associate degree in Engineering from SOWELA Technical College, an Associate degree in Occupational Safety and Health from McNeese State University, a Bachelor's degree in Occupational Safety and Health from Southeastern Louisiana University, and a Master's degree in Safety Management from Indiana State University. Mike has also been a licensed Home and Commercial Inspector since 2013. He is currently employed as a Safety Engineer with LyondellBasell Industries.
